Karada is a comprehensive platform designed to accelerate your development workflow by seamlessly bridging the gap between traditional REST APIs and modern AI agents. Whether you're a solo developer shipping a new app or a massive team managing complex microservices, Karada provides the infrastructure to deploy, scale, and integrate faster.

## Why Karada?

In the era of AI-driven development, tools like Claude, Cursor, and ChatGPT need to interact with your data. Traditionally, this meant writing custom wrapper code for every single API.

Karada solves this by providing **Auto-MCP**: an automated pipeline that converts standard OpenAPI and Swagger specifications into fully-functional TypeScript servers implementing the [Model Context Protocol (MCP)](https://modelcontextprotocol.io).
