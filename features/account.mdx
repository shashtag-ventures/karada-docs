---

Your account settings control your personal identity on the Karada platform. Access them by clicking your avatar in the top-right corner and selecting **Account Settings**.

## Profile

### Avatar

Your avatar is displayed next to your name across the platform — in team lists, deployment logs, and comments. Click on your existing avatar to upload a new one from your device.

<Note>
  An avatar is optional but strongly recommended for team collaboration.
</Note>

### Display Name

Set a display name that other team members will see. This can be your full name or any alias you prefer.

- Maximum **32 characters**.
- Click **Save** to apply changes.

### Email

Your primary email is used for:

- **Login authentication** (via OAuth)
- **Account-related notifications** (deployment alerts, team invitations)

Your email is displayed with a **Primary** badge and cannot currently be changed through the UI. Contact support if you need to update it.

---

## Authentication

Karada uses **OAuth 2.0** for authentication. You sign in using a third-party provider (such as Google or GitHub), which means:

- **No passwords to manage** — authentication is handled by your identity provider.
- **Automatic session management** — Karada securely manages your session cookies.
- **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)** — Enable 2FA on your identity provider (e.g., GitHub) for an additional layer of security on your Karada account.

---

## Billing

The **Billing** section allows workspace owners to manage their subscription plan and payment methods. Billing is always associated with a **Workspace**, not an individual account.

To view or update billing:

1. Navigate to your **Account Settings**.
2. Select the **Billing** tab in the sidebar.

---

## Danger Zone

At the bottom of your account settings, you'll find the **Delete Account** section.

<Warning>
  Deleting your account is **permanent and irreversible**. This action will:
  - Remove your personal workspace and all its projects
  - Revoke your access to any team workspaces
  - Delete all associated deployment history

You will be asked to confirm before the deletion proceeds.

</Warning>
