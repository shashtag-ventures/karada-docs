Karada is built for collaboration from day one. Teams allow multiple users to manage and monitor the same projects together.

## Managing Team Members

1. Navigate to your Team Settings using the dropdown in the top-left corner.
2. Click **Members**.
3. Invite new users by providing their email address.

## Roles and Permissions

Currently, Karada supports single-level access, meaning any member invited to a team has full access to create, edit, and delete projects within that team.

## Personal Teams

When you sign up for Karada, a personal team is automatically created for you. You can use this for your independent projects, and create dedicated organizations for your collaborative work.
